## Brand Story

**EZLY** is the enterprise platform that transforms how homeowners and contractors connect. We believe that quality home improvement should be simple, transparent, and built on real relationships—not transactions.

Our mission: **Building Trust, One Project at a Time**

We make it easy for homeowners to find vetted contractors and for contractors to grow their business through genuine connections.

### The Problem We Solve
- Homeowners struggle to find trustworthy contractors
- Contractors waste time on unqualified leads
- Communication is fragmented across platforms
- No transparent way to track projects and manage relationships

### Our Solution
- A unified platform connecting verified professionals with homeowners
- Real-time communication and project management
- Transparent bidding and contract systems
- Enterprise-grade security and reliability


## Imagery & Photography

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic:** Clean, professional, authentic, community-focused

#### Guidelines:
- Real people (not stock photos where possible)
- Natural lighting, warm tones
- Project before/after comparisons
- Contractor portraits showing expertise
- Homeowners in their spaces
- Team collaboration moments

#### What To Avoid:
- Overly staged, artificial studio shots
- Generic stock photography
- Overly filtered or unrealistic colors
- Cluttered compositions
- Anything that looks inauthentic

### Illustration Style

**Aesthetic:** Modern, geometric, minimalist

#### Usage:
- Onboarding flows
- Empty states
- Feature explanations
- Error states

#### Style Guide:
- Geometric shapes (circles, squares, curves)
- Limited color palette (use brand colors + neutrals)
- Consistent line weights (2-4px)
- Friendly but professional tone

### Icon System

**Style:** Lucide Icons (consistent with existing implementation)

**Guidelines:**
- 24px for UI buttons
- 32px for larger illustrations
- 48px+ for feature highlights
- Always use brand colors or neutral grays
- Maintain consistent stroke weight (2px)

## Logo Usage

### Logo Versions

#### Primary Logo (Horizontal)
```
Usage: Websites, documents, primary brand presence
Minimum Size: 200px width
Clear Space: 20px on all sides
```

#### Logo Icon (Square)
```
Usage: App icons, favicons, social media avatars
Minimum Size: 48px
Clear Space: 12px on all sides
```

#### Logo Mark (Symbol Only)
```
Usage: Mobile app, compact spaces, buttons
Minimum Size: 32px
Clear Space: 8px on all sides
```

### Logo Color Variations

#### Full Color (Primary)
```
Used on: Light backgrounds
Colors: Navy Blue + Teal
```

#### Single Color - Navy
```
Used on: Documents, prints, monochrome
Color: Navy Blue (#0F3A7D)
```

#### White/Knockout
```
Used on: Dark backgrounds, headers
Color: White (#FFFFFF)
```

### Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around logo equal to the height of the letter "E" in the wordmark.

### Minimum Sizes

```
Logo Horizontal: 200px minimum width
Logo Square: 48px minimum width
Logo Icon: 32px minimum width
```

### What NOT To Do

❌ Stretch or distort the logo
❌ Add effects (shadows, glows, 3D)
❌ Change colors arbitrarily
❌ Rotate or skew the logo
❌ Use on complex backgrounds without proper contrast
❌ Remove the clearspace
❌ Combine with other logos or text

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics

**Professional + Warm + Knowledgeable**

- Use clear, simple language
- Be helpful, not preachy
- Show genuine care for outcomes
- Be confident without being arrogant
- Use active voice
- Focus on benefits, not features

### Tone Variations

#### Friendly & Approachable (Homepage, Marketing)
"Let's find you the perfect contractor"
"Building trust, one project at a time"

#### Professional & Direct (Dashboard, CTAs)
"Submit Your Bid"
"Accept Project"
"View Contract"

#### Supportive & Helpful (Help, Onboarding)
"Here's how to create your account..."
"We're here to help you find success"

#### Confident & Action-Oriented (Confirmations)
"Project posted successfully!"
"Your bid was accepted!"

### Key Messaging Pillars

1. **Trust** - "We verify every contractor"
2. **Community** - "Real relationships drive real results"
3. **Simplicity** - "No complexity. Just results."
4. **Quality** - "Enterprise-grade for every project"

### Words to Use

✓ Simple, clear language
✓ Active voice
✓ Specific details
✓ Benefit-focused
✓ Genuine, authentic
✓ Action-oriented

### Words to Avoid

✗ Jargon or technical terms
✗ Passive voice
✗ Vague statements
✗ Hype or overselling
✗ Condescending tone
✗ Industry buzzwords

## Do's & Don'ts

### DO ✅

- Use the color palette consistently
- Maintain clear space around logos
- Use the approved fonts
- Keep layouts clean and spacious
- Use real, authentic imagery
- Be consistent with messaging
- Test for accessibility
- Use proper contrast ratios
- Maintain professional quality
- Update materials when guidelines change

### DON'T ❌

- Mix fonts from different families
- Use outdated brand materials
- Create new color variations
- Add effects to logos
- Use low-quality images
- Be inconsistent with messaging
- Forget about mobile responsiveness
- Use deprecated logo versions
- Make unauthorized changes
- Ignore accessibility standards
